THE GEORGIA YOU MAY NOT KNOW ABOUT  

You may have heard of the US state of Georgia located at its eastern coast, but did you know that there's a country nestled within the Caucasus mountains which bears the same name? It comes as no surprise that the US state of Georgia gains more recognition worldwide, given that the nation of Georgia is one of half the size and one third the population of its American name-twin.

If this has piqued your interest, here's what you need to know about the beautiful country of Georgia.

THE IMPORTANT MAPS

GEORGIA'S TOURISM SCENE

In 2018, Georgia was visited 4.757 million times by international tourists, making it the 58th most visited country and countributing 0.35% to world tourism statistics. With all that Georgia has to offer, it certainly deserves a spot higher up in the list, alongside the Frances and the USAs of the world.

The line chart on the right compares Georgia's inbound tourism numbers with those of its neighbouring countries.

The donut chart tells us that Georgians largely prefer neighbouring countries when holidaying abroad. That said, the majority of Georgian citizens actually choose to travel domestically, a testament to Georgia's charm and allure.

This begs the question: is Georgia expensive? Quite the opposite, actually. In 2019, the Georgian capital of Tbilisi was ranked as the 49th cheapest city in the world. See how Tbilisi stacks up against other major cities in terms of expenses in the bar charts above.
